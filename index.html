The word is . You are the Spy!

Next Game » End Game

To play the game: One player doesn't know the word - they are the spy. Players take turns saying a related word trying to prove their innocence, but they can't give the word away. If the group guesses the spy correctly before either the time runs out or the spy guesses the word, they win - else the spy wins!
